IceCore Dynasty starts with a vision.

## More Than One Season

Most fantasy leagues end when the playoffs are over.

Players are forgotten.

Teams are reset.

Everything starts from zero.

We wanted something different.

IceCore Dynasty is built around long-term franchise management, where every decision matters not only today, but years into the future.

## Think Like a General Manager

Managing a dynasty means much more than setting your lineup.

You'll scout prospects.

Manage your Salary Cap.

Plan future drafts.

Build organizational depth.

Negotiate trades.

Every move shapes your franchise.

## Built for the Future

This website is only the beginning.

Our long-term vision includes:

- Team Pages
- Hall of Fame
- Historical Records
- Draft Archive
- League Statistics
- AI-powered News Portal
- Salary Cap Tracker

We want IceCore Dynasty to become more than a fantasy league.

We want it to become a long-term hockey community.

## Our Philosophy

Winning a championship is an achievement.

Building a dynasty is a legacy.

Welcome to IceCore Dynasty.
