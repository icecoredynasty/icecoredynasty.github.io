Yahoo Fantasy may not offer every feature available on the market.
That was never our goal.

<!--more-->

## Simplicity Comes First

There are fantasy platforms with countless settings, advanced contracts, detailed finances and complex roster management.

Those systems can be impressive, but they also require a significant time investment.

IceCore Dynasty follows a different philosophy.

We believe fantasy hockey should remain fun.

You shouldn't have to spend hours learning the platform before enjoying your team.

Yahoo provides a clean, reliable and familiar experience that lets every manager focus on what really matters — building a successful franchise.

## Building Our Own World

Instead of relying on every built-in feature, we decided to create our own league systems.

Salary Cap.

Offline Free Agency.

Coin values.

Team pages.

Historical records.

League news.

Hall of Fame.

Prospect tracking.

By combining Yahoo Fantasy with our own rules and website, we can build a unique hockey universe while keeping the platform simple and accessible.

## Inspired by the NHL

IceCore Dynasty isn't trying to copy the NHL.

It's inspired by it.

General Managers will still draft young talent, manage salaries, negotiate trades and make long-term decisions.

Every season becomes another chapter in the history of the league.

Our own history.

Our own champions.

Our own legends.

## The IceCore Coin System

Instead of replicating real NHL contracts, IceCore uses its own virtual currency.

Every player has a value expressed in **Coins**, creating a flexible salary cap that is easy to understand and balanced for fantasy hockey.

The Coin system allows us to reward breakout players, adjust market values and introduce strategic roster decisions without adding unnecessary complexity.

Simple to learn.

Challenging to master.

## Looking Forward

This website is only the beginning.

Our long-term vision includes advanced team pages, statistics, AI-generated articles, historical archives, scouting reports and many other tools designed specifically for IceCore Dynasty.

Yahoo gives us a solid foundation.

Everything else, we build ourselves.

## Our Philosophy

Fantasy hockey should reward knowledge.

Dynasty should reward patience.

IceCore rewards both.

Welcome to the next generation of fantasy franchise building.
