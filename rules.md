<h1>League Rules</h1>

<p>
IceCore Dynasty is built around long-term franchise management, strategic planning and active participation.
Our goal is to create a competitive but friendly environment where General Managers can build successful organizations over many seasons.
</p>

<h2>League Format</h2>

<ul>
<li>12 General Managers</li>
<li>Yahoo Fantasy Hockey</li>
<li>Head-to-Head Categories</li>
<li>Dynasty League</li>
<li>Free to Join</li>
</ul>

<h2>Salary Cap</h2>

<p>
Every franchise operates under a salary cap of <strong>300 Coins</strong>.
Player values are updated each offseason based on league rules.
Managing contracts and cap space is one of the key strategic elements of IceCore Dynasty.
</p>

<h2>Keepers</h2>

<p>
Each offseason every team submits its protected players before the official keeper deadline.
All remaining players return to the draft pool.
Keeper rules may evolve as the league grows.
</p>

<h2>Draft</h2>

<p>
IceCore Dynasty uses an annual live draft together with a rookie draft.
Draft order is determined according to league rules and previous season results.
</p>

<h2>Trading</h2>

<p>
Trading is encouraged throughout the season.
Managers are expected to negotiate in good faith and avoid collusion.
Commissioners reserve the right to review exceptional cases.
</p>

<h2>Activity</h2>

<p>
IceCore Dynasty is designed for active General Managers.
Regular lineup updates, communication and participation are expected from every member.
Inactive teams may eventually be replaced.
</p>

<h2>Sportsmanship</h2>

<p>
Respect between managers always comes first.
Competition should be intense on the ice and respectful outside of it.
</p>

<p>
Additional rules may be updated before each new season.
</p>
