<h1>Join IceCore Dynasty</h1>

<p>
IceCore Dynasty is a competitive Yahoo Fantasy Hockey Dynasty League built for General Managers who enjoy long-term strategy, salary cap management and franchise building.
More than a single season, we focus on creating a league that grows year after year through active managers, smart decisions and a strong community.
</p>

<h2>Why Join?</h2>

<ul>
<li>🏒 Active and committed General Managers</li>
<li>💰 300 Coin Salary Cap System</li>
<li>🏆 True Dynasty League Format</li>
<li>🌟 Annual Rookie Draft</li>
<li>📋 Offline Free Agency</li>
<li>📰 League News & AI-powered Portal</li>
<li>📈 Long-term team building and history</li>
</ul>

<h2>League Philosophy</h2>

<p>
Winning a championship is an achievement.
Building a dynasty is a legacy.
</p>

<ul>
<li>Build your franchise over many seasons.</li>
<li>Trade wisely.</li>
<li>Develop future stars.</li>
<li>Think long-term.</li>
<li>Create your legacy.</li>
</ul>
